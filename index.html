Featured Research 2026

When Kalman Filters Meet Neural Networks

Vehicle Localization Under Sensor Failure

Clean GPS Kalman: 2.65m NN: 7.67m
Noisy (5m) Kalman: 3.62m NN: 7.77m
Dropout 10% Kalman: 2.82m NN: 7.75m
Urban Canyon Kalman: 10.19m NN: 12.62m

Comparative study of classical Kalman filtering versus learned neural fusion for autonomous vehicle localization. Tested on 4,661 KITTI frames under four degradation conditions. Kalman wins accuracy; the neural network provides uncertainty estimates the Kalman filter cannot, but reveals a calibration failure in Monte Carlo Dropout under sensor degradation.

Full Research Paper | KITTI Odometry Dataset | Python, PyTorch
